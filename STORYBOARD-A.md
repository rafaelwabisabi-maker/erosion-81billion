# STORYBOARD VERSION A — "THE TICKER"
### EROSION: 81 BILLION | Video Documentary | ~3 minutes

> **Concept:** Cold open on the counter. No context. No explanation. Pure accumulation.
> The horror builds before the viewer understands why. Then the frame breaks — human voices enter.
> Then the tool appears. Art → Accountability → Action.
> 
> **Tone:** Urgent. Monumental. Unflinching. Ends with radical simplicity.
> **Music:** Slow, droning low strings (cello) → silence → white noise → silence

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

**To produce this video you need:**
1. Screen recording of the live demo (OBS or QuickTime)
2. Photo sequence: import Pexels images, add text overlays (CapCut, Canva, or DaVinci Resolve)
3. Voice recording: 3 voices + narrator (ElevenLabs or real recording)
4. Assembly: 10 scenes, total 3 minutes, minimal cut rhythm
5. Export: MP4 H264, 1920×1080, stereo audio

**Time estimate:** 2–3 hours with the right tools.
**Recommended:** Record the demo first, build around it.
