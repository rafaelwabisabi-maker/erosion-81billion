# STORYBOARD VERSION B — "THE EXCLUDED"
### EROSION: 81 BILLION | Video Documentary | ~3 minutes

> **Concept:** Starts with a single face. One person. One story. Then zooms out — slowly,
> relentlessly — to civilizational scale. The counter appears mid-film, not at the start.
> By then, the viewer has already bonded with the human. The scale hits harder.
> Ends with radical hope: the tool in use. Light after the dark.
>
> **Tone:** Intimate → devastating → quietly hopeful. A documentary feel.
> **Structural arc:** Human → System → Failure → Scale → Tool → Call to action
> **Music:** Single piano, unresolved chord → silence → drone → resolution

## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR VERSION B

**Pacing note:** Version B breathes more. The cuts are longer. Trust the human faces.

**Key difference from A:** The counter is a *reveal*, not the opening. By the time it appears, the viewer has already been emotionally prepared. The impact is greater.

**Voice casting note:** If using ElevenLabs or similar:
- Scene 1/2/8 voice: `Matilda` or `Aria` (warm, elderly female, European)
- Scene 6 voice: `Adam` (warm, elderly male)
- Narrator: `Daniel` (neutral, authoritative, British/European)

**Music note:** A single unresolved piano note is more powerful than a full score. The resolution in Scene 10 is earned.
